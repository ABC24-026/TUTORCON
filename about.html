About TutorCon

We make it easier to get help when you need it most.

TutorCon started because finding a good tutor was harder than it should be. We built the platform we wished existed — one that connects students and tutors across any institution, quickly and reliably.

Our Mission

No student should fail for lack of the right help.

The reality for many students in Gaborone and surrounding areas is that finding tutoring for their specific course — at their institution — can be nearly impossible. Some subjects have only a handful of students. Some campuses offer no centralised support. And talented tutors rely on WhatsApp posts that reach almost nobody.

TutorCon changes that. We built a single platform where students can browse, compare, and book verified tutors from any institution — and where tutors can build a real profile and get steady bookings without chasing anyone.

It works across UB, BUAN, Botho, BAC, ABM, BCET, Limkokwing, and secondary schools in the region.

The Problem We Solve

Why students struggle to find the right tutor

These are the real challenges we set out to fix.

🔎

Too few tutors per subject

Niche courses have almost no tutors available at a single campus. Students hit a wall before they even start looking.

🏫

No centralised support

Most institutions don't offer organised tutoring. Students are left to figure it out themselves with no guidance.

📱

WhatsApp isn't enough

Tutors post on group chats and hope someone sees it. Great tutors go undiscovered. Students miss the help they need.

🏝️

Stuck within your campus

Even if a perfect tutor exists at a nearby institution, there's no way to find or access them without TutorCon.

What TutorCon Offers

Everything a student or tutor actually needs

One platform. All the tools. No friction.

👤

Verified tutor profiles

Every tutor is verified by institution. Students see real pass rates, subjects, availability, and honest student reviews.

🔀

Cross-institution matching

A BUAN student can access a UB tutor for the same module. No more being limited to your own campus.

📅

Real booking flow

Pick a date, time, and session type. Get a confirmation page with a reference number. No more "let me check and get back to you."

💬

In-platform messaging

Message tutors directly on TutorCon. No lost WhatsApp threads, no awkward cold texts to strangers.

📚

Study material marketplace

Tutors can sell notes and study guides on their profiles — extra help outside session hours.

⭐

Real reviews and ratings

Students choose tutors based on verified reviews and star ratings. No more guessing if a tutor is actually good.
