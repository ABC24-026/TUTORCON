About TutorCon

We're Solving Academic Access in Gaborone

TutorCon was born out of a simple frustration: students couldn't find the academic help they needed, and skilled tutors couldn't reach students who needed them. We built the bridge.

Our Mission

Every Student Deserves Access to Quality Tutoring

In Gaborone's institutions, some courses are pursued by very few students, making it nearly impossible to find a tutor within your own campus. Meanwhile, talented tutors advertise on WhatsApp with no reach beyond their immediate circle.

TutorCon is a digital platform purpose-built for Gaborone's academic community — connecting students and tutors across BAC, BUAN, UB, Botho, ABM, BCET, Limkokwing and secondary schools through one trusted platform.

Our mixed-tutoring model means a BUAN student can access a UB tutor for the same BSc module — removing institutional boundaries from academic success.

The Problem We Solve

Why Students Struggle to Find Help

These are the real academic access challenges TutorCon was built to address.

🔎

Rare Course Tutors

Some courses are pursued by very few students, meaning qualified tutors for those subjects are nearly impossible to find on campus.

🏫

No Centralised Services

Most Gaborone institutions don't offer centralised tutoring services, leaving students to fend for themselves with no support structure.

📱

WhatsApp Word-of-Mouth

Tutors rely on WhatsApp and word-of-mouth advertising that doesn't scale — great tutors go undiscovered, and students miss out.

🏝️

Institution Silos

Students are locked into tutors from their own institution, even when expert tutors for their specific modules exist at another university nearby.

Our Solution

What TutorCon Offers

A full digital ecosystem for academic support in Gaborone — built for both students and tutors.

👤

Verified Tutor Profiles

Every tutor is verified by institution. Students see real pass rates, subject lists, session types, and honest student reviews.

🔀

Cross-Institution Matching

Our unique mixed-tutoring model lets students access tutors from any Gaborone institution — not just their own campus.

📅

Seamless Booking

Browse, filter, and book tutors for part-time or full-time sessions directly through the platform — no more chasing numbers.

💬

In-Platform Messaging

All communication happens on TutorCon. No lost WhatsApp threads, no missed appointments.

📚

Study Material Marketplace

Tutors can optionally sell study notes and materials, creating an additional income stream while helping students study independently.

⭐

Ratings & Reviews

Students choose tutors based on real ratings, pass rates, and verified reviews — building a culture of academic excellence.
